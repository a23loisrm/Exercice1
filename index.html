Lucanus cervus

Information basic

The lucanus deer (Lucanus cervus), also called flying deer or European stag beetle, is a species of scarab beetle belonging to the family Lucanidae. It is the largest beetle in Europe. Adults are seen from mid-June to mid-August, with June being the month with the highest number of observations. In mid-June they begin to emerge from the base of the trees or from areas where the decaying wood is soft, in July and early August is mating, and in mid-August the breeding season ends. They are not a pest, as they only eat decaying wood and do not attack healthy trees.
